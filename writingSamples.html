Mr. Left

Mr. Left loved to write.

He would practice each day, and each night.

'Till his left hand was sore, he'd write a bit more,

but his writing was never alright.

Dear Cake

Dear Cake,
You were once so delicious and moist,
now I don't know who you are.

Some days your frosting was soft and sweet,
and sometimes your cherries were tart and cold.

What happened to you cake?
Once so full of life, now you’re just old and stale.

I wished you’d give your heart to me,
but you won’t even give me a piece.
.
No matter though, we're through,
I have found pie.

Nathan

Pain is a Patient Friend

The ache that sits above one's shoulders
can solve all problems but itself.

Each scar loves company,
its neighbors wait in earnest.

Pressure, the vice,
silence screams its name

Comfort lies in the facade,
a smile is easily stretched.

Everyday

My arms are spread wide,

flight, caressed by spilt milk skies.

beep beep beep beep beep.
